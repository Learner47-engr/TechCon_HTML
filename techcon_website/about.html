History

TechCon began in 1995 as a small, informal gathering of Silicon Valley engineers sharing nascent internet innovations. Initially called "NetNexus," it quickly outgrew its garage origins. By 2000, rebranded as TechCon, it embraced emerging mobile and software trends, attracting wider industry attention.

The 2010s saw explosive growth, pivoting to AI, cloud computing, and big data. TechCon became a global phenomenon, renowned for groundbreaking keynotes and fostering vital industry connections. Today, it remains a premier event, continually evolving to showcase the bleeding edge of technological advancement and its societal impact

Mission

TechCon's core goal is to accelerate technological advancement and its positive societal impact. Our driving principles revolve around knowledge sharing, collaboration, and inspiration. We aim to connect diverse stakeholders – from cutting-edge innovators and entrepreneurs to investors and policymakers – fostering an environment where bold ideas are exchanged, partnerships forged, and challenges tackled collectively. By showcasing transformative technologies and real-world applications, TechCon empowers attendees to drive economic growth, enhance governance, and contribute to a more sustainable future, continually pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
